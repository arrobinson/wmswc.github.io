About Us

Hey there we're the Society of Women in Computing. SWC is our fancypants name for the ACM-W chapter at Wiliiam & Mary. I just wanted to see what this would look like.ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women.
